Our Project

The Student Career Development System was created by our Team to allows students to create professional resumes as they transition from school life to work life.

This system is designed to make it easy for the user to generate a ready to use resume by following and providing the system with their educational background and previous work history.

We encourage our user to take advantage of the many different tools provided within our system to help them find the right career.
